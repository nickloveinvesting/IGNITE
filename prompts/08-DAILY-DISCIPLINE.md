## Artifact 5: pdf-template-specs.md

### PDF Overview
- **Title:** The 90-Day Discipline Tracker
- **Pages:** 20-25 pages
- **Format:** 8.5" x 11", portrait (printable)
- **Design:** Clean, functional, tracker-focused

### Page Breakdown

**Page 1: Cover**
- Title: "90-Day Discipline Tracker"
- Subtitle: "Build Unstoppable Consistency"
- IGNITE branding

**Page 2: How to Use This Tracker**
- Instructions:
  1. Choose 5 daily habits (non-negotiables)
  2. Check off each habit every day
  3. Track your streak
  4. Don't break the chain for 90 days
- Tips for success

**Page 3: Choose Your 5 Daily Habits**
- Prompts to help them choose:
  - Physical: Workout, walk, stretch
  - Mental: Read, journal, meditate
  - Business: Outreach, content, sales calls
  - Personal: No phone before 9am, gratitude practice
- Space to write their 5 habits

**Page 4: Habit Stacking Guide**
- "How to Make Habits Automatic"
- James Clear's habit stacking formula: "After [CURRENT HABIT], I will [NEW HABIT]"
- Examples:
  - After I pour my coffee, I will journal for 5 minutes
  - After I brush my teeth, I will do 10 push-ups
- Space to design their habit stacks

**Page 5: Morning Routine Template**
- Sample morning routine
- Space to design their own

**Page 6: Evening Routine Template**
- Sample evening routine
- Space to design their own

**Page 7: Accountability Contract**
- "I commit to tracking my 5 habits every day for 90 days."
- "I will not break the chain."
- Signature line, date

**Pages 8-22: Daily Tracker Pages (90 days, 6 days per page)**

Each tracker page includes:
- Week number
- 6 days (M, T, W, Th, F, Sa/Su combined row for weekend)
- 5 columns for 5 habits
- Checkbox for each habit/day
- "Streak" counter at bottom of page
- Motivational quote

**Example Tracker Page:**

```

Day | Habit 1 | Habit 2 | Habit 3 | Habit 4 | Habit 5 | Total

Tue |    □    |    □    |    □    |    □    |    □    | 0/5
Wed |    □    |    □    |    □    |    □    |    □    | 0/5
Thu |    □    |    □    |    □    |    □    |    □    | 0/5
Fri |    □    |    □    |    □    |    □    |    □    | 0/5
Sat |    □    |    □    |    □    |    □    |    □    | 0/5

Current Streak: ___ days
Longest Streak: ___ days

Quote: "Discipline is choosing between what you want now and what you want most."
```
