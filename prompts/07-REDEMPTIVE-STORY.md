## Artifact 5: pdf-template-specs.md

### PDF Overview
- **Title:** The Redemptive Story Framework: Turn Your Failure Into Fuel
- **Pages:** 14-18 pages
- **Format:** 8.5" x 11", portrait
- **Design:** Hopeful, forward-focused, redemptive aesthetic

### Page Breakdown

**Page 1: Cover**
- Title, subtitle, IGNITE branding

**Page 2: Introduction**
- "Your past isn't a prison. It's proof."
- Why redemptive stories matter
- How to use this framework

**Page 3-4: Examples of Redemptive Stories**
- Steve Jobs: Fired from Apple → Came back, created iPhone
- Sara Blakely: Failed LSAT, rejected from law school → Created Spanx, became billionaire
- Walt Disney: Fired for "lack of imagination," went bankrupt → Built Disney empire
- Oprah Winfrey: Abusive childhood, fired from TV job → Media mogul
- "Your story is next."

**Page 5: The 5-Part Redemptive Story Framework**

**Part 1: The Fall**
- What happened? (The failure, loss, mistake)
- How did you feel? (Shame, fear, regret)
- What did you lose? (Money, status, relationships)

**Part 2: The Lesson**
- What did this teach you?
- What did you learn about yourself?
- What had to die for you to grow?

**Part 3: The Rising**
- How did you get back up?
- What action did you take?
- What kept you going?

**Part 4: The Transformation**
- Who did you become through this?
- How are you different now?
- What strengths did you gain?

**Part 5: The Mission**
- How do you use this experience to help others?
- What's your message now?
- Who are you serving because of what you went through?
