## COMMON PITFALLS & SOLUTIONS

### Pitfall #1: Low Landing Page Conversion (<15%)

**Likely Causes:**
- Headline doesn't resonate
- Too much friction (long form)
- Not enough trust signals
- Video doesn't play/slow load

**Solutions:**
- A/B test headline variations
- Reduce form to name + email only
- Add more testimonials/research citations
- Optimize video player, reduce file size

### Pitfall #2: Low Worksheet Completion (<50%)

**Likely Causes:**
- Worksheet too long/overwhelming
- Instructions unclear
- No implementation support
- No follow-up encouragement

**Solutions:**
- Add clear time estimate (20-30 min)
- Create two-stage structure (Levels 1-3, then 4-7)
- Emphasize implementation planning section
- Send encouragement email Day 2

### Pitfall #3: High Cost Per Lead (>$12)

**Likely Causes:**
- Poor ad targeting
- Weak ad creative/copy
- Wrong audience
- Low landing page conversion (compounding issue)

**Solutions:**
- Refine audience (narrower targeting)
- Test new ad angles (empathy vs. pattern interrupt)
- Create video ads (often better CTR)
- Fix landing page first (doubles ad effectiveness)

### Pitfall #4: Low Lead → Client Conversion (<3%)

**Likely Causes:**
- Wrong audience (tire-kickers, not buyers)
- No clear path to coaching
- Weak email nurture
- Offer mismatch

**Solutions:**
- Qualify leads better (add quiz/survey)
- Create clear coaching CTA in Email #4, #8
- Strengthen implementation emails (show need for support)
- Ensure coaching offer aligns with deep why clarity
