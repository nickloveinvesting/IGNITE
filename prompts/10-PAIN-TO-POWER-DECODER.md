## Artifact 2: quiz-structure.md

### Decoder Overview

**Structure:** Two-part assessment
- Part 1: "The Pain" (Where you were)
- Part 2: "The Power" (Where you are now)
- Output: Visual before/after comparison with transformation insights

**Question Format:** Likert scale (1-5) + open-ended reflection
**Time to Complete:** 10-12 minutes
**Delivery:** On-screen visual decoder + email with downloadable graphic

---

### Part 1: The Pain (Where You Were)

**Instructions:** "Think back to where you were 6-12 months ago (or before you started your transformation journey). Rate each statement honestly."

**Pain Assessment Questions (10 questions, rate 1-5):**

1. "I felt stuck in my current situation with no clear way out." (1 = strongly disagree, 5 = strongly agree)
2. "I lacked clarity on what I wanted in life." (1-5)
3. "I struggled with self-doubt and imposter syndrome." (1-5)
4. "I made excuses instead of taking action." (1-5)
5. "I felt financially trapped or stressed." (1-5)
6. "I lacked confidence in my abilities." (1-5)
7. "I consumed content but rarely took action." (1-5)
8. "I felt isolated and without support." (1-5)
9. "I doubted whether I could achieve my goals." (1-5)
10. "I felt like I was wasting my potential." (1-5)

**Pain Score:** Sum of all ratings (10-50 scale)
- 40-50: Extreme pain/struggle
- 30-39: Significant pain
- 20-29: Moderate pain
- 10-19: Mild pain

**Open-Ended Pain Reflection:**
"In one sentence, describe your biggest pain point from that time:"
[Text box]

---

### Part 2: The Power (Where You Are Now)

**Instructions:** "Now rate where you are TODAY. Be honest about your current state."

**Power Assessment Questions (10 questions, rate 1-5):**

1. "I have clarity on my goals and vision for the future." (1 = strongly disagree, 5 = strongly agree)
2. "I take consistent action toward my goals." (1-5)
3. "I believe in my ability to succeed." (1-5)
4. "I make decisions quickly and confidently." (1-5)
5. "I invest in myself (time, money, energy)." (1-5)
6. "I have a supportive community or network." (1-5)
7. "I see failure as learning, not defeat." (1-5)
8. "I prioritize high-impact actions over busy work." (1-5)
9. "I feel like I'm becoming the person I want to be." (1-5)
10. "I'm building real momentum toward my goals." (1-5)

**Power Score:** Sum of all ratings (10-50 scale)
- 40-50: High power/transformation
- 30-39: Strong progress
- 20-29: Moderate progress
- 10-19: Early stages

**Open-Ended Power Reflection:**
"In one sentence, describe your biggest win or shift:"
[Text box]

---

### Transformation Calculation

**Transformation Score = Power Score - Pain Score**

Possible range: -40 to +40

**Transformation Categories:**

**+30 to +40: Massive Transformation**
- "You've undergone a profound transformation. You're a completely different person than you were."

**+20 to +29: Significant Transformation**
- "You've made major progress. You're on a clear upward trajectory."

**+10 to +19: Solid Transformation**
- "You've made real progress. You're moving in the right direction."

**0 to +9: Early Transformation**
- "You're starting to shift. Keep going - momentum is building."

**-10 to -1: Stagnant (pain still high, power low)**
- "You're aware of your pain but haven't fully stepped into your power yet. That's about to change."
