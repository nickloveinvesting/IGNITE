## Customization Guidelines

### For Different Audiences

**W2 Employees:**
- Emphasize freedom, escape, autonomy
- Softer challenge, more empathy
- "You deserve more" messaging

**1099 Contractors:**
- Emphasize stability, systems, predictability
- "Stop the feast-famine cycle"
- Focus on business building

**Entrepreneurs:**
- Emphasize growth, scale, next level
- Harder challenge, high expectations
- "You're leaving money on the table"

### Testing Strategy

1. **Week 1:** Test all 5 variations at $30/day each
2. **Week 2:** Pause bottom 2, increase top 3 to $50/day
3. **Week 3:** Pause bottom 1, increase top 2 to $100/day
4. **Week 4:** Scale winner to $200+/day, keep #2 at $50/day

### Compliance Notes

**Avoid:**
- "You" language in cold traffic (use "people", "most", etc.)
- Guaranteed results or specific income claims
- Before/after health transformations
- Targeting based on sensitive categories

**Required:**
- Disclaimer if making any claims
- Brand name somewhere in ad
- Clear expectation of what they're getting

---

**Adapt this framework for each magnet. Test multiple angles. Scale winners. Pause losers. Optimize continuously.**
