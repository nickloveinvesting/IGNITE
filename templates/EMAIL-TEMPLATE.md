# Email Sequence Template

Use this template to create email sequences for new lead magnets.

---

## Email 1: Welcome + Deliver

**Send:** Immediately after opt-in

**Subject:** [Welcome + Set Expectations]
Example: "Your [MAGNET NAME] is here (check your spam folder)"

**Preview Text:** [Don't repeat subject - add value]
Example: "Plus: what to expect from me over the next 2 weeks"

**Body:**
```
Hey [FIRST_NAME],

Your [MAGNET NAME] is attached / linked below.

[DOWNLOAD LINK]

Over the next [X] days, I'm going to send you [X] emails about [TOPIC].

No fluff. No BS. Just what you need to [ACHIEVE OUTCOME].

If you don't want these emails, unsubscribe. I won't be offended.

But if you're serious about [GOAL], stick around.

- Toby

P.S. [Quick instruction or next step]
```

## Customization Notes

**Voice Adjustments:**
- Keep Toby Potter's direct, no-nonsense tone
- Adjust intensity based on audience segment
- W2 employees: More empathy, less aggression
- Entrepreneurs: More challenge, higher expectations

**Timing Adjustments:**
- Compressed sequence: 7 days total (1 email per day)
- Standard sequence: 12 days (email every 1-2 days)
- Extended sequence: 21 days (email every 2-3 days)

**Segmentation:**
- Tag based on engagement (opens, clicks, replies)
- Create branches based on quiz results or behavior
- Send different Email 5-7 based on segment

---

**Use this template as starting point. Customize for specific magnet and audience. Test and optimize based on open/click rates.**
