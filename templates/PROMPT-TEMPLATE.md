## IGNITE BRAND GUIDELINES

### Colors
- **Primary:** Fire Orange (#FF4D00) - CTAs, headlines, urgency
- **Secondary:** Charcoal (#2C3539) - Body text, backgrounds, structure
- **Tertiary:** White (#FFFFFF) - Backgrounds, contrast, breathing room

### Typography
- **Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Weights:** 400 (Regular), 600 (Semi-Bold), 700 (Bold)
- **Hierarchy:** H1: 48px Bold, H2: 36px Semi-Bold, H3: 24px Semi-Bold, Body: 16px Regular, CTA: 18px Bold

### Voice: Toby Potter Style
- **Direct:** No fluff, get to the point immediately
- **Authoritative:** Speak with conviction, no hedging
- **Action-Oriented:** Focus on what to DO, not just know
- **No-Nonsense:** Call out excuses, demand responsibility
- **Empowering:** Build people up while pushing them forward

**Avoid:** Corporate jargon, passive voice, hedging language, excessive politeness
