# SECTION E: 5-QUESTION QUIZ STRUCTURE

## Quiz Architecture & Scoring Logic

### Question 1: Archetype Identification

**Question:** "Which archetype resonates most with how you operate?"

**Options:**
- **A) The Visionary** - You see the big picture, set bold visions, inspire others. Your challenge: staying grounded in daily execution.
  - *Practice Focus:* Clarity, vision casting, strategic thinking

- **B) The Executor** - You get things done, drive results, focus on efficiency. Your challenge: sometimes missing the bigger why.
  - *Practice Focus:* Implementation, execution, progress tracking

- **C) The Connector** - You build relationships, lead teams, create culture. Your challenge: not losing yourself in others' needs.
  - *Practice Focus:* Relationship depth, personal boundaries, authentic connection

- **D) The Creator** - You innovate, generate ideas, solve problems creatively. Your challenge: finishing what you start.
  - *Practice Focus:* Creative expression, idea capture, artistic development

- **E) The Optimizer** - You improve systems, refine processes, eliminate waste. Your challenge: sometimes over-engineering.
  - *Practice Focus:* Systems thinking, continuous improvement, efficiency gains

**Scoring:** Each archetype maps to specific practice types (meditation, journaling, action-based, creative, analytical).

### Question 2: Time Availability

**Question:** "How much uninterrupted time can you realistically commit to daily practice?"

**Options:**
- **15 minutes** - "I'm packed, need something quick and mighty"
  - Blueprint: Single focused practice (choose one: journaling, 10-min meditation, 5-min breathing + 10-min visualization)

- **30 minutes** - "I can carve this out if it's the right priority"
  - Blueprint: 2-3 combined practices (meditation + journaling, or breathwork + journaling + intention-setting)

- **60 minutes** - "I'm protective of this morning/evening time"
  - Blueprint: Full-spectrum practice (breathwork + meditation + movement + journaling + strategic thinking)

**Scoring:** Duration determines practice structure and complexity level.

### Question 3: Primary Goal/Transformation

**Question:** "What's the #1 transformation you want in the next 90 days?"

**Options:**
- **A) Leadership Clarity** - "I need to be a clearer, more confident leader"
  - Practice Elements: Vision clarity, presence, decision confidence, team influence

- **B) Revenue & Impact** - "I want to scale my business and increase income"
  - Practice Elements: Goal clarity, action priority, growth mindset, opportunity spotting

- **C) Deep Relationships** - "I want stronger, more authentic connections in my life"
  - Practice Elements: Emotional intelligence, vulnerability, active listening, boundary-setting

- **D) Creative Breakthrough** - "I want to unlock my creative potential"
  - Practice Elements: Idea generation, inspiration capture, creative blocks, artistic expression

- **E) Personal Peace** - "I want less stress, more calm, better balance"
  - Practice Elements: Anxiety management, sleep quality, nervous system regulation, presence

**Scoring:** Goal maps to specific practice recommendations and blueprint structure.

### Question 4: Time-of-Day Preference

**Question:** "When are you most likely to actually DO this practice?"

**Options:**
- **A) Early morning (5-7am)** - "I'm a natural early riser or willing to become one"
  - Blueprint: Energizing practices (movement, breathing, intention-setting, visualization)
  - Sends timing: 5:30am reminders

- **B) Midday (12-1pm)** - "I can do this during my lunch break or between meetings"
  - Blueprint: Centering practices (meditation, journaling, breathing, strategic planning)
  - Email timing: 11:45am reminder

- **C) Evening (6-9pm)** - "After work is my best time, before bed"
  - Blueprint: Reflective practices (journaling, meditation, review, gratitude)
  - Email timing: 6pm reminder

**Scoring:** Timing preference determines email send times and practice recommendations (chronotype personalization).

### Question 5: Email & Contact

**Question:** "What's your email? We'll send your blueprint and daily practice reminders."

**Fields:**
- Email address (required for delivery)
- First name (required for personalization)
- Optional: Phone number (for SMS reminders - future feature)

**Scoring:** Captures contact info; optional phone enables SMS variant testing.

## Troubleshooting Guide

### Common Issues & Solutions

**Issue: Blueprint PDFs not generating**
- Solution: Check PDF generation service has enough API credits
- Solution: Verify all personalization variables are populating correctly
- Solution: Test with plain-text version first, then add formatting

**Issue: Emails not sending on schedule**
- Solution: Verify email platform timezone settings match user timezone
- Solution: Check that automation rules have correct "Wait" duration
- Solution: Ensure email templates are marked as "Ready to Send" (not Draft)

**Issue: Quiz branching logic not working**
- Solution: Clear browser cache and test incognito window
- Solution: Verify conditional logic rules in quiz platform
- Solution: Test with multiple browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)

**Issue: Low quiz completion rate (<60%)**
- Solution: Reduce question complexity or clarity issues
- Solution: A/B test question wording
- Solution: Check mobile experience (ensure buttons are clickable)

**Issue: High email unsubscribes after Email 1**
- Solution: Review welcome email tone (may feel too salesy)
- Solution: Verify email from name and subject line expectations
- Solution: Check for spam triggers (all caps, excessive exclamation marks)

**Issue: Low click-through on Email 2-7**
- Solution: Verify email is rendering correctly in preview
- Solution: Ensure CTAs are above the fold
- Solution: Check that personalization variables are populating (not showing {brackets})
