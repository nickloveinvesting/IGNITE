# Team Roles & Responsibilities

This document defines the roles, responsibilities, and decision-making authority for executing the IGNITE Lead Magnets system.

---

## Team Structure

The lead magnet system requires coordination across multiple roles. Depending on team size, one person may fill multiple roles.

---

## Core Roles

### 1. Prompt Engineer / Lead Magnet Strategist

**Primary Responsibility:** Design and refine lead magnet strategies and prompts

**Key Duties:**
- ✅ Research target audience pain points and psychology
- ✅ Design lead magnet frameworks and positioning
- ✅ Write/refine Claude prompts in `/prompts/` folder
- ✅ Ensure prompts generate complete, high-quality artifacts
- ✅ Iterate on prompts based on performance data
- ✅ Document magnet strategy and research findings

**Decision Authority:**
- Approve magnet concepts and angles
- Decide which research findings inform prompt design
- Approve final prompt before artifact generation

**Success Metrics:**
- All 10 magnets have complete, tested prompts
- Magnet conversion rates exceed benchmarks
- Research findings incorporated into copy
- Prompts generate quality artifacts (90%+ usability rate)

**Required Skills:**
- Marketing psychology
- Copywriting
- Research synthesis
- Claude AI prompt expertise
- IGNITE positioning knowledge

**Time Commitment:** 15-20 hours/week

**Team Members:** Toby Potter (primary), Market Research Analyst

## Onboarding New Team Members

### For Prompt Engineers
1. Read `/docs/BRAND_GUIDELINES.md` (1 hour)
2. Review `/docs/RESEARCH_FRAMEWORK.md` (2 hours)
3. Study `/research/` folder findings (3 hours)
4. Review existing prompts in `/prompts/` (3 hours)
5. Create a test prompt (4 hours)
6. Get feedback and iterate (2 hours)

### For Artifact Managers
1. Learn repository structure (1 hour)
2. Review naming conventions in CONTRIBUTING.md (1 hour)
3. Study `/docs/QUALITY_CHECKLIST.md` (2 hours)
4. Organize a test set of artifacts (3 hours)
5. Get feedback and establish workflow (2 hours)

### For Other Roles
1. Read role description and responsibilities (1 hour)
2. Shadow current role holder (4 hours)
3. Review past performance data (2 hours)
4. Complete first task with oversight (3-5 hours)
5. Debrief and discuss learnings (1 hour)

## Performance Evaluation

Each role is evaluated quarterly on:

1. **On-time Delivery**: % of deadlines met
2. **Quality**: QA score, customer satisfaction
3. **Collaboration**: Team feedback, communication
4. **Impact**: Revenue contribution, conversion improvements
5. **Growth**: Skills developed, initiative shown
