## OVERVIEW

This checklist ensures all assets for the No Excuses Mandate Builder lead magnet are production-ready, brand-compliant, and optimized for conversion before launch.

**Review Frequency:**
- Initial review: Before first launch
- Re-review: After any significant changes
- Periodic audit: Monthly (once live)

**Responsibility:**
- Marketing Manager: Strategic review
- Designer: Visual compliance
- Copywriter: Voice/tone compliance

- QA Tester: End-to-end user experience
