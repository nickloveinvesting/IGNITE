## CONTINGENCY PLANS

### Scenario 1: High CPL (>$20)

**Diagnosis:**
- Ad creative not resonating (low CTR)
- Targeting too broad or too narrow
- Landing page not converting (high bounce rate)

**Solution:**
- Test new ad creatives (different hooks, angles)
- Narrow targeting (exclude low-intent audiences)
- A/B test landing page headline and CTA
- Consider reducing ad budget until CPL improves

---

### Scenario 2: Low Conversion Rate (<20%)

**Diagnosis:**
- Landing page not compelling (weak copy, unclear value)
- Traffic quality poor (wrong audience)
- Technical issues (slow load, broken form)

**Solution:**
- Run heat map analysis (see where visitors drop off)
- Test shorter form (email only, no first name)
- Add video above the fold (increase engagement)
- Test different trust signals (testimonials, guarantees)

---

### Scenario 3: Low Call Booking Rate (<15%)

**Diagnosis:**
- Leads not qualified (freebie seekers, not buyers)
- Email sequence not compelling (low engagement)
- CTA to book call not clear or strong enough

**Solution:**
- Add qualification question to landing page form ("What's your #1 goal?")
- Strengthen Email #3 (make IGNITE benefits clearer)
- Test different CTA language ("Schedule Your Free Strategy Call" vs. "Let's Talk About IGNITE")
- Add urgency (limited spots, calendar scarcity)

---

### Scenario 4: Ad Fatigue (CTR Drops Below 2%)

**Diagnosis:**
- Same creative shown too many times to same audience
- Audience saturation (reached everyone interested)

**Solution:**
- Refresh ad creatives (new images, videos, copy)
- Expand to new audiences (lookalikes, interests)
- Reduce frequency (cap at 2-3 impressions per user per week)
- Rotate ads regularly (new creative every 7-14 days)
