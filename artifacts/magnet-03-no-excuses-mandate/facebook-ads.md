## A/B TESTING FRAMEWORK

### Test Variables (One at a time)

**Week 1: Angle Test**
- Run all 3 variations (A, B, C) equally
- Measure: CTR, CPL, landing page conversion
- Winner: Best CPL + lead quality

**Week 2: Creative Test (Using winning angle)**
- Test: Image vs. Video
- Test: Different image concepts
- Measure: CTR, CPL, engagement rate

**Week 3: Headline Test**
- Test 3-5 different headlines
- Keep creative constant
- Measure: CTR

**Week 4: Audience Test**
- Test different audience segments
- Keep ad constant
- Measure: CPL, lead quality score
