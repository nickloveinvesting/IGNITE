## PRODUCTION SPECIFICATIONS

### **CAMERA SETUP:**

**Equipment:**
- **Camera:** DSLR, mirrorless, or high-quality smartphone (iPhone 12+ or equivalent)
- **Lens:** 35mm-50mm equivalent (flattering for talking head)
- **Tripod:** Stable, eye-level positioning
- **Lighting:** 3-point lighting or ring light (soft, flattering, no harsh shadows)
- **Microphone:** Lavalier or shotgun mic (clear audio is critical)

**Framing:**
- Rule of thirds (subject slightly off-center)
- Eye level (not looking down or up at camera)
- Clean background (minimalist, not distractingâ€”bookshelf, plants, or plain wall)

---

### **VISUAL SETTINGS:**

**Resolution:** 1080p minimum (4K preferred for future-proofing)
**Frame Rate:** 24fps (cinematic) or 30fps (standard)
**Aspect Ratios:**
