## CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE & BUDGETING

### **Campaign Setup:**

**Campaign Level:**
- **Objective:** Conversions (optimize for lead generation)
- **Budget:** $50-100/day total (split across ad sets)

**Ad Set Level (3 Ad Sets - One Per Creative Variation):**
- **Ad Set 1:** The Confrontation ($20-30/day)
- **Ad Set 2:** Famous Failures Hook ($20-30/day)
- **Ad Set 3:** Pain-to-Purpose Reframe ($20-30/day)

**Ad Level:**
- Each ad set contains 2-3 creative variations (image vs. video, different copy)
- Let Facebook test and optimize

### **Optimization Strategy:**

**Days 1-3:**
- Monitor CPL (cost per lead)
- Check landing page conversion rate
- Ensure pixel tracking correctly

**Days 4-7:**
- Identify winning ad sets (lowest CPL, highest conversion)
- Pause underperformers (CPL >$10 or conversion <25%)
- Increase budget on winners by 20%

**Week 2+:**
- Scale winning ads (increase budget 20% every 3-4 days)
- Create lookalike audiences from converters
- Launch retargeting campaign for non-converters
