## RISK MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Email Deliverability Issues

**Mitigation:**
- Proper domain warm-up (2-4 weeks)
- Authenticate sending domain (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- Monitor spam complaint rates (<0.1%)
- Remove hard bounces immediately
- Maintain engagement focus (remove non-openers after 90 days)

**Backup Plan:**
- Use established sending domain (not brand new)
- Consider transactional email service (higher deliverability)
- Monitor with tools (GlockApps, Mail Tester)

---

### Risk 2: Facebook Ad Rejection

**Mitigation:**
- Follow ad policies strictly (no personal attributes, health claims)
- Land page matches ad claims exactly
- Avoid sensational language
- Use clear, non-misleading copy

**Backup Plan:**
- Prepare 2-3 ad copy variations (approved alternates)
- Appeal rejections immediately
- Have Google Ads campaign ready as backup

---

### Risk 3: Low Challenge Completion Rate (<50%)

**Mitigation:**
- Front-load value (Day 1 must deliver)
- Keep emails short and actionable (50-75 words Days 2-28)
- Gamify progress ("You're 50% through!")
- Add community element (Facebook group?)

**Backup Plan:**
- Send re-engagement at Day 7 for non-openers
- Survey drop-offs to understand why
- Consider reducing to 14-day or 21-day challenge

---

### Risk 4: Budget Overrun Without ROI

**Mitigation:**
- Set hard CPA limit ($15 max)
- Pause underperforming ads daily
- Only scale ads with CPA <$10
- Track LTV:CAC ratio (target 3:1 minimum)

**Backup Plan:**
- Reduce ad spend to $25-50/day (test mode)
- Focus on organic traffic (free)
- Drive existing email list to challenge
