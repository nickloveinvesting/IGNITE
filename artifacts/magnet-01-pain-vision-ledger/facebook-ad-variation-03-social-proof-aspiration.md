## IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS FOR CANVA

**Dimensions**: 1200 x 628 pixels (Facebook feed optimal size)

### Design Layout:
- **Background**: Charcoal (#121212) — solid background
- **Theme**: Testimonial + social proof style

**LEFT SIDE (40% of image)**:
- **Testimonial Photo**: Professional headshot
  - Woman, 30s-40s, professional attire
  - Smiling, confident, relatable
  - High-quality photo (stock or real testimonial)
  - Circular frame with fire gradient border (#C84A00 → #FF6A00)

- **Star Rating**: Above photo
  - 4-5 stars (★★★★★)
  - Gold/orange color (#FF6A00)
  - Size: Medium, clearly visible

- **Quote Overlay**: Below photo
  - Text: "This changed how I make decisions"
  - Attribution: "— Sarah M., VP Marketing"
  - Font: Inter Regular, 18px (quote), 14px (attribution)
  - Color: White (#FFFFFF)
  - Italicized quote, regular attribution

**RIGHT SIDE (60% of image)**:
- **Stats Section**: Clean, vertical list
  - Stat 1: "1,200+ Used It"
    - Number in fire orange (#FF6A00)
    - Inter Bold, 48px for number
    - Inter Regular, 20px for text

  - Stat 2: "87% Moved To Action"
    - Same styling as Stat 1

  - Stat 3: "9.1/10 Clarity"
    - Same styling as Stat 1

- **Visual Accent**: Fire orange vertical line separating left/right
- **Background**: Subtle texture or gradient on stats side

**DIVIDER**:
- Vertical fire gradient line (#C84A00 → #FF6A00) between photo and stats
- 4px width

**OVERALL STYLE**:
- Modern testimonial design
- Professional, trustworthy
- Social proof-forward
- Clean, uncluttered

**TEXT PERCENTAGE**:
- Keep text under 20% of image
- Stats are minimal, high-impact

**MOOD**: Aspirational, trustworthy, results-focused, social proof
