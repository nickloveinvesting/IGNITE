## IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS FOR CANVA

**Dimensions**: 1200 x 628 pixels (Facebook feed optimal size)

### Design Layout:
- **Background**: Charcoal (#121212) with subtle gradient to Rich Black (#0A0A0A)
- **Split Design**: Vertical split down the middle

**LEFT SIDE (Problem State)**:
- Blurred, tangled lines or abstract chaos imagery
- Dark, heavy atmosphere
- Represents "stuck" or "confused" state
- Low saturation, darker tones

**RIGHT SIDE (Clarity State)**:
- Clean, organized lines or clear pathway
- Lighter atmosphere (still on-brand dark, but clearer)
- Represents "clarity" or "vision"
- Slightly higher saturation, fire orange accents

**DIVIDER**:
- Vertical fire gradient line (#C84A00 → #FF6A00) separating left and right
- Arrow pointing from left → right (transformation)

**TEXT OVERLAY**:
- Position: Center, overlaying both sides
- Text: "SEE YOUR COST"
- Font: Inter Bold, 72px
- Color: White (#FFFFFF)
- Stroke: 2px dark outline for readability

**COLOR ACCENTS**:
- Fire orange (#FF6A00) accent line at top edge
- Subtle glow effect around text

**TEXT PERCENTAGE**:
- Keep text under 20% of image (Facebook's recommendation)
- Minimal text, maximum visual impact

**MOOD**: Professional, clean, modern, authoritative
