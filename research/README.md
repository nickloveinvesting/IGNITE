# Research

Competitive analysis, audience research, market trends, platform optimization, and case studies.

---

## Purpose

This folder stores all research outputs that inform lead magnet strategy, copywriting, design, and optimization decisions.

---

## Files in This Folder

### COMPETITOR-ANALYSIS.md
**Purpose:** Analyze how industry leaders create and promote lead magnets
**Targets:** Russell Brunson, Tony Robbins, Alex Hormozi, Brendon Burchard, Grant Cardone, Dean Graziosi
**Updates:** Quarterly or when major competitor launches

### AUDIENCE-RESEARCH.md
**Purpose:** Deep-dive into W2, 1099, and Entrepreneur psychographics
**Contents:** Pain points, desires, objections, triggers, language patterns
**Updates:** Annually or when significant market shifts occur

### MARKET-TRENDS.md
**Purpose:** Track current lead magnet trends and format innovations
**Contents:** What's working in 2024+, emerging formats, platform changes
**Updates:** Quarterly

### PLATFORM-OPTIMIZATION.md
**Purpose:** Best practices for Leadpages, Typeform, Facebook, email platforms
**Contents:** Conversion optimization, technical requirements, algorithm updates
**Updates:** As platforms update

### CASE-STUDIES.md
**Purpose:** Real examples of successful lead magnets with performance data
**Contents:** Format, conversion rates, what made them work, lessons learned
**Updates:** As new data becomes available

## How Research Is Used

### During Prompt Creation
- Competitive intelligence informs differentiation
- Audience psychology informs messaging
- Platform optimization informs technical specs

### During Artifact Customization
- Headline testing based on competitive analysis
- Copy refinement based on audience language patterns
- Design optimization based on platform best practices

### During Optimization
- Performance comparisons against benchmarks
- A/B test ideas from case studies
- Troubleshooting using platform research

---

## Research Process

### Competitive Intelligence
1. Sign up for competitor lead magnets
2. Document funnel structure
3. Analyze copy, design, sequencing
4. Identify strengths, weaknesses, gaps
5. Extract lessons for IGNITE

### Audience Research
1. Monitor Reddit, Facebook groups, Quora
2. Interview IGNITE members
3. Analyze email replies and objections
4. Track language patterns
5. Map pain points and triggers

### Platform Optimization
1. Follow platform blogs and updates
2. Test new features and formats
3. Analyze performance data
4. Document what works vs. what doesn't
5. Update best practices
