## TOP 10 MISTAKES

1. ❌ **Generic titles** - "Guide to Social Media Marketing"
   ✅ **Specific titles** - "The 10-Post Formula That Grew My Following from 200 to 20K in 90 Days"

2. ❌ **No pain point addressed** - "The History of Stock Markets"
   ✅ **Clear pain point** - "How to Invest Without Losing Sleep Over Market Crashes"

3. ❌ **Rushed title creation** - Spending 5 minutes on it
   ✅ **Time investment** - Spend as much time as content creation

4. ❌ **Overpromising** - "Double Your Income in 30 Days (Guaranteed)"
   ✅ **Realistic promise** - "The 30-Day Plan That Grew My Revenue 47%"

5. ❌ **Too broad** - "The Complete Guide to Building a Successful Online Business"
   ✅ **Focused scope** - "The 5-Step Launch Sequence: $0 to $10K/Month"

6. ❌ **No visual** - Text-only offering
   ✅ **With mockup** - 3D cover/box visual (5-20X conversion lift)

7. ❌ **No testing** - Using first title written
   ✅ **A/B testing** - Test 2-3 variations

8. ❌ **No curiosity gap** - "Use These 3 Templates: [shows all templates]"
   ✅ **Curiosity preserved** - "The 3 Email Templates That Book Calls on Autopilot"

9. ❌ **Wrong format** - 50-page ebook for busy audience
   ✅ **Format match** - Quick checklist or 1-page template

10. ❌ **No segmentation** - Same title for everyone
    ✅ **Segment-specific** - 3-5 variations for different audiences
