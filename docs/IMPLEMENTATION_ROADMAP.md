**Total Timeline:** 4 weeks (aggressive) to 8 weeks (conservative)

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Speed of execution** - Launch all 10 magnets within 3-4 weeks
2. **Data-driven decisions** - Track everything, optimize based on data
3. **Team coordination** - Daily standups, clear communication
4. **Quality maintenance** - Don't sacrifice quality for speed

---

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk:** Ad account suspended
**Mitigation:** Have backup ad account, follow Facebook policies strictly

**Risk:** Email deliverability issues
**Mitigation:** Warm up domain, maintain <0.1% spam complaints, use double opt-in

**Risk:** Landing page builder downtime
**Mitigation:** Have backup HTML versions hosted independently

**Risk:** Low conversion rates
**Mitigation:** A/B test aggressively, optimize based on data, consult experts

**Risk:** Team bandwidth constraints
**Mitigation:** Prioritize top performers, hire freelancers as needed, use templates
