# COMPETITIVE RESEARCH - QUICK REFERENCE SUMMARY

## TOP 6 COMPETITIVE ANGLES FOR 3-PART FUNNELS

### 1. EPIPHANY BRIDGE (Russell Brunson)
**Concept:** Lead with emotional breakthrough, support with logic
**Structure:** Backstory → Desire → Conflict → Epiphany → Plan → Achievement → Transformation
**Key Quote:** "When they have that epiphany, they will come seeking the logical explanations"

### 2. SIDEWAYS SALES LETTER (Jeff Walker)
**Concept:** Problem-Solution-Problem over 7-10 days
**Pre-Launch:** 3-4 emails with video/content over 7-10 days
**Open Cart:** 4-7 days with FOMO-driven close
**Result:** Builds anticipation without overwhelming

### 3. CHALLENGE SEQUENCES (Brendon Burchard)
**Concept:** 7-day guided course with daily action
**Strategy:** Convert to small customer early (Day 2 tripwire), then ascend to main offer
**Power:** "Most powerful launch to engage brand new audience"

### 4. RPM FRAMEWORK (Tony Robbins)
**Concept:** Results + Purpose + Massive Action Plan
**Application:** Email 1 = Results clarity, Email 2 = Purpose connection, Email 3 = Action plan
**Philosophy:** "If you have enough reasons, you can get yourself to do anything"

### 5. PAIN-CLAIM-GAIN
**Concept:** Complete decision journey framework
**Sequence:** Why Change? (Pain) → Why You? (Claim) → Why Now? (Gain)
**Evolution:** Balance pain with empowerment

### 6. INTERACTIVE ASSESSMENT
**Conversion:** 70% vs. 36% for passive content
**Case Study:** $96,401 in 3 months, 20.22% conversion (10X increase)
**Examples:** ProActiv (20M+ completions), RealAge (40M+ completions)
