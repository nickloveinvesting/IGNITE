## ðŸ“ž SUPPORT & TROUBLESHOOTING

### Common Issues & Solutions

**Issue:** Form not submitting
- Check email platform connection
- Verify form field names match platform requirements
- Test with different browsers
- Check for JavaScript errors in console

**Issue:** Emails not sending
- Verify automation is turned ON
- Check trigger conditions
- Ensure subscriber is not in "unsubscribed" list
- Verify send time settings (timezone)

**Issue:** Low email open rates
- Check spam score (Mail-Tester.com)
- Authenticate domain (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- Test different subject lines
- Avoid spam trigger words

**Issue:** High unsubscribe rate (>2%)
- Messaging may be too aggressive
- Check email frequency (too many emails too fast)
- Verify expectations set on landing page match email content
- Add more value in emails (less selling, more teaching)

**Issue:** Facebook ads rejected
- Text overlay >20% of image (reduce text)
- Misleading claims (revise copy)
- Landing page doesn't match ad promise (align messaging)
- Prohibited content (check Facebook ad policies)

**Issue:** High cost per lead
- Narrow targeting (too broad)
- Improve landing page conversion (optimize page)
- Test new ad creatives (current ones not resonating)
- Lower budget (sometimes higher budget = higher costs in testing phase)
